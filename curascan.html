Core Capabilities

Built for Real Clinical Needs

From automated scan reports to counterfeit drug alerts — CuraScan brings together AI diagnostics and medicine safety in one secure platform.

🧠

AI Scan Analysis

Convolutional Neural Networks trained on NIH/RSNA datasets detect structural anomalies in X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans with over 85% target accuracy.

📋

Automated Preliminary Reports

The moment a scan is analyzed, a structured draft report is generated automatically — giving doctors a head start and reducing diagnostic delays.

💊

OCR Medicine Authentication

Point your camera at any medicine packaging. Google ML Kit reads the label, cross-checks against trusted pharmaceutical databases, and flags counterfeits.

⚠️

Drug Interaction Alerts

Scan multiple medicines and CuraScan checks for dangerous Drug-Drug Interactions (DDI), displaying safety alerts before harm can occur.

📶

Offline-First Medicine Checks

Rural and low-connectivity areas are no barrier. Medicine verification runs on-device using a local SQLite database and offline OCR — no internet needed.

🔐

Role-Based Access Control

Separate secure dashboards for Patients, Doctors, and Administrators ensure that sensitive scan data is only visible to authorized personnel.

Workflow

Scan to Report in Four Steps

A seamless pipeline from image upload to doctor-reviewed diagnosis.

1

Upload Scan

Patient uploads X-ray, MRI, or CT scan image through the mobile app or web portal.

2

AI Analysis

The CNN model preprocesses the image, detects anomalies, and generates a confidence score.

3

Report Generation

A structured preliminary medical report is auto-generated and securely stored in MongoDB.

4

Doctor Review

The assigned doctor reviews, annotates, and finalizes the report. Patient is notified instantly.

User Portals

Designed for Every Role

Three distinct, secure dashboards — tailored to how each user actually works.

🧑‍⚕️

Patient

  • Upload X-ray, MRI, or CT scans
  • View AI-generated reports and history
  • Scan medicines for authenticity
  • Check drug interaction safety
  • Receive notifications on scan status
  • Download reports as PDF
👨‍💼

Doctor

  • Review AI preliminary reports
  • Add comments and clinical insights
  • Approve or request re-analysis
  • Manage patient scan history
  • View patient scan images
  • Finalize and sign-off reports
🛡️

Administrator

  • Manage all user accounts
  • Monitor system activity and logs
  • Manage medical records and scans
  • Configure system settings
  • Oversee platform usage metrics
  • Ensure data integrity and security
